Twenty One Pilots

Welcome to my Twenty One Pilots fanpage! I love this band because not only does their music speak to me on so many levels, the detail and creativity that goes into the cryptic stories and even creating a whole world with characters that they use in reference to mental health, faith and mortality is incredible. Their music found me and got me through a lot when I was going through a rough time in my life and since then they've always been my favorite band. The two members Tyler Joeseph and Josh Dun put a lot of thought and passion in their music and you can really feel it when you listen to them. Another thing I really like about them is that their songs don't all sound the same. Even though a lot of their songs have a distinct upbeat rythym followed by sadder lyrics for the most part every song is still unique in it's own way. Their music style is interesting. Even being described as "ukulele screamo" which I could not agree with more. It's hard to pick any one favorite song of theirs since they're all so good but Stressed Out was the first song I heard by them so that holds a special place in my heart as well as Lane Boy. And while all of their albums are amazing with so much thought behind each one my favorite albums would have to be Blurryface and Breach.

About Twenty One Pilots

Coming out of Columbus, Ohio, this band features two people; the singer Tyler Joseph and the drummer Josh Dun. The band was formed in 2009 with two other original members that left the band in 2011 and since then it's just been the two of them. Their band name 'Twenty One Pilots' was inspired by All My Sons - a play written by Arthur Miller about the moral dilemma faced by a man who knowingly sold faulty warplane parts in World War II causing the death of 21 pilots.
